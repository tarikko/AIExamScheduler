Analytical Notes

Heuristics & Lessons
  • Greedy uses Largest Enrollment + Degree (Graph Coloring analogy).
  • CSP relies on MRV + Forward Checking to prune the most conflicting exams early.
  • Genetic Algorithm swaps weeks (subsets of slots) and favors penalty minimization.
  • A* treats each partial schedule as a state with heuristic = remaining exams × conflict density.
Comparative Evaluation
  • All strategies stop when 0 hard violations are found; soft penalties drive room gaps.
  • Execution time listed in milliseconds; every strategy runs in the browser.
  • Visual grid highlights room utilization; empty cells mean unconstrained slots.
